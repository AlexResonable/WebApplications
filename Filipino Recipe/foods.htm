Foods

Picture Name Description
Adobo: Meat or vegetable that is marinated in garlic, soy sauce, and vinegar.
Afritada: Beef cooked with tomato sauce or tomato paste.
Arrozcaldo: Rice porridge usually with chicken.
Asado: Braised meat in soy sauce and brown sugar.
Bopis: A spicy dish made out of internal organs.
Crispy Pata: Deep fried pork legs.
Daing: Dried fish.
Dinengdeng: Boiled vegetables with bagoong (fermented paste made from shrimp or small fish).
Dinuguan: Internal organs cooked with blood.
Ginataan: Cooked with coconut milk (gata).
Giniling: Ground meat cooked with soy sauce and vegetables.
Inasal: Grilled marinated meat.
Kaldereta: Meat cooked with tomato sauce or tomato paste with liver spread.
Kare-Kare: Ox tail stew cooked in peanut sauce.
Lechon: Roasted whole pig.
Longganiza: Sausage.
Lumpia: Spring roll.
Mami: Noodle soup.
Nilaga: Meat stew with vegetables.
Paksiw: Fish cooked with vinegar.
Pancit: Stir fried noodles.
Pinakbet: Vegetable dish that is cooked with bagoong.
Sinigang: Meat or fish stew cooked with a sour ingredient usually tamarind.
Tapsilog: A breakfast combination of tapa (dried meat), sinangag (fried rice), and itlog (fried egg).
Tinola: Chicken stew usually with papaya or malungay leaves.
Torta: Omelet.
